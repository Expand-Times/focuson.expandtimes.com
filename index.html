Privacy Policy

App Name: Focus On: Minimalist Launcher

Short Description: Minimal Looking Phone Launcher for Screen Time Limit and Digital Detox

Effective Date: March 15, 2026


1. Information We Collect

Focus On: Minimalist Launcher does not collect any personal data. We respect your privacy and ensure that all information remains on your device.


2. Local Storage

Any information, such as your screen time usage, app settings, and customisations, is stored locally on your phone. This data is never transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.


3. Permissions

The app only requests permissions necessary to function as a launcher. No additional personal information, such as contacts, location, or online activity, is accessed.


4. Third-Party Services

Focus On does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or cloud services. All features operate fully offline.


5. Data Security

All data stored on your device is managed according to standard Android security practices. We do not store or transmit your information externally.


6. Children’s Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages. Since no personal data is collected, we do not knowingly gather information from children.
